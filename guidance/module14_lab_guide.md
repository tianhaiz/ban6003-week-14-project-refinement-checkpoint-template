## What You Will Practice

You will draft or revise the executive summary, business problem, data source description, profiling summary, cleaning and transformation summary, integration and ABT explanation, data dictionary notes, ABT checks, model or analytical method, results interpretation, limitations, ethics, recommendations, and presentation storyline.

## Recommended Workflow

1. Open the notebook in Codespaces or local Jupyter.
2. Work through the project refinement prompts.
3. Revisit your ABT, documentation, and analysis results.
4. Strengthen explanations that are vague or unsupported.
5. Check that limitations and ethics are included.
6. Draft a clear final presentation storyline.
7. Save your work, then commit and push if using GitHub.

## Final Project Balance

The final project should not be only a modeling report. Make the data workflow visible: profiling, cleaning, transformation, integration, ABT construction, validation, and documentation should connect to the later analysis.

## Minimum Completion Checklist

Before submitting, make sure:

- You explained your data sources.
- You summarized profiling, cleaning, transformation, and integration.
- You stated the ABT unit of analysis.
- You described data dictionary and validation work.
- You explained your analytical method and results.
- You included limitations, ethics, and recommendations.
- You drafted a final presentation storyline.
